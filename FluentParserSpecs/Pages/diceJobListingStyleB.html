Company Overview

Rightpoint is an award-winning digital agency and technology consulting firm. The company brings industry-focused business strategy and technology expertise together with user experience design and creative services to help organizations transform the way they do business.

Rightpoint's digital marketing solutions create brand advocacy and enhance the customer experience through digital channels to accelerate business growth. Rightpoint's enterprise solutions improve operational performance and increase employee productivity within an organization to drive increased profitability. Rightpoint delivers world-class business solutions in areas such as collaboration, social business, CRM, web, digital strategy, mobile, business intelligence, and cloud. The company is deeply rooted in a collaborative, “intrapreneurial” culture with a passion to “WOW” clients.

Based in Chicago, we attract highly qualified professionals with the right combination of business, strategy, creative, and technical skills. Our consultants participate in the growth of an energetic, team-oriented, and entrepreneurial-minded firm with challenging consulting engagements. We are passionately driven to make a personal impact on both the growth and success of the company and our clients. Recent accolades include being named one of the Top 20 Best Places to Work in Chicago in 2013 by Crain's Chicago Business, one of the Seven Small Jewels in 2013 by Consulting Magazine, and among Chicago’s Top 101 Best and Brightest Companies to Work For in 2012.

Job Description

The .NET Developer/Solutions Consultant will be responsible for building and maintaining internal and external facing web applications for Rightpoint's clients. This person will work with a team (of approximately 3-8 people) of experienced project managers, user experience consultants, visual designers, analysts, developers, and business resources to build enterprise systems that improve business productivity and support operations.  It is important that the .NET Developer/Solutions Consultant have a strong background in building scalable, maintainable, high-quality, and high performing web applications using the Microsoft technology stack.

 

Sample Responsibilities

  • Develop features in .NET custom applications and .NET components of SharePoint solutions based on feature specifications and technical designs.
  • Contribute to creating feature specifications and technical design schematics.
  • Contribute to requirements gathering and analysis activities such as storyboarding, developing use cases, and creating functional requirements specifications.
  • Create database models and build out models using SQL Server, then surface data to user interfaces by using object oriented techniques, often using object-to-relational mappers.
  • Create web user interfaces using HTML, CSS, and Javascript (specifically jQuery).
  • Create web user interface server-side functionality using ASP.NET Web Forms, ASP.NET MVC, and SharePoint.
  • Take responsibility for feature-focused unit and system testing (built both by you and others).
  • Contribute to facilitating acceptance testing procedures and monitoring results.
  • Contribute to performance and load testing procedures and issue remediation.

Required Education

  • Bachelor of Science in Business/Computer Science or related field.

Required Skills and Abilities

  • Solid understanding of object-oriented programming (OOP) and computer science foundations.
  • Experience with designing relational databases and optimizing database structure for performance and/or maintainability and the ability to design and optimize stored procedures.
  • Experience with Test-Driven Development.
  • Experience with source control, work item tracking, and build management with Team Foundation Server (or similar)
  • Deep hands-on knowledge of the .NET 3.5/4.0/4.5 Framework, including Visual Studio 2008/2010/2012, C#, ASP.NET, ADO.Net, jQuery, and CSS.
  • Strong experience working with n-tier architectures (UI, Business Logic Layer, Data Access Layer) and experience with service-oriented architectures (SOA).
  • Experience with Object Relational Modeling (ORM) with different tools such as Entity Framework (and/or nHibernate) and Linq.
  • Hands-on experience building scalable web applications, in particular building high frequency and low latency web applications with optimal memory management, performance, and scalability as a key focus.
  • Candidate should be a self-motivated, independent, detail oriented, responsible team-player and exhibit exceptional relationship management skills.
  • Passion for building high-quality systems with software implementation best practices.
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
  • Knowledge of sound business practices and a proven track record of anticipating and exceeding customer expectations.
  • Must be motivated with a focus on team performance.
  • Prior consulting experience is desired.
  • Exposure to project management experiences is a plus.
  • Experience in development and implementations using Microsoft SharePoint is a huge plus.
  • SQL Reporting Services or DBA experience a plus.
  • Willingness to travel to clients located throughout the Chicagoland area on a regular basis and a willingness to occasionally travel to client sites located throughout the United States on an occasional basis.

What’s it like to work at Rightpoint?

Recently Crain’s ranked Rightpoint among the “Top 20 Best Places to Work in Chicago.” Why? In large part because Rightpoint walks the talk in term of its culture. Rightpoint has a set of 7 values and uses them as a compass. In addition, Rightpointers have passion for their work. They want to build something and enjoy solving complex problems every day in a collaborative environment. Our founders wanted to foster a culture where team members feel challenged yet love what they are doing so much so that they envision Rightpoint being the last stop in their career.

We hire A-Players because we are working on cutting-edge projects and our clients expect solutions that will WOW them. We help some of Chicago’s top businesses find solutions to their digital needs. After we deliver our solutions, we ask our clients a very basic question. On a scale of 1-10, “How likely would you be to refer us to a friend or colleague?” – We strive for an average of 9, and are currently exceeding that goal. We ask the same question of our employees: “On a scale of 1-10, how likely are you to recommend Rightpoint to a friend as a place to work?” And, we’re proud to say that our average score exceeds 9 on that front too.

Why do the best want to work here? The reasons vary but, for many, it’s because they are challenged by their work and because they get to work with some of the most influential companies in Chicago. The icing on the cake is that they also have a great time doing it! We take our work very seriously but ourselves? Not so much. At Rightpoint’s offices, conference rooms have names like Hickory, Dickory, and Dock and we have a “WOW” Wall where Rightpointers and our clients can recognize accomplishments of colleagues by filling out a “Holy Sheet!”

What are some other things that add to the texture of Rightpoint? We thrive to give back to the community and have a Compassion Crew that organizes regular events. Our Culture Club ensures that our culture is engrained in everything we do and we stay consistent with our values. Oh, and did we mention we have a Keg in our kitchen where we gather around most Fridays for a “Rightpint” Happy Hour? And we cater in lunch every other Friday? Those are just a few examples of what makes Rightpoint a truly great place to work.
