LEAD RUBY ENGINEER NEEDED FOR FUNDED STARTUP (DENVER)

3455 Ringsby Ct.
Are you interested in leading or being on a small team that develops amazing code and makes a difference in the world? We are a funded, growing, Denver-based startup that is taking a big step forward! We've got a shipping product, customers and are expanding our product line to disrupt the education space. The new stuff's not even on our website yet, but we already have over $100K in orders from just a prototype!!!

WHAT WE ARE LOOKING FOR:
We are bringing all engineering in-house and need two RoRs developers to head up that area of our business and direct our future products. We are a RoR shop, so you need 2-3 years of experience. We are working with an outsourced UI/UX team and open to partnering with off-shore development for heavy load periods, if necessary. You'll need real-world experience with Postgres SQL databases, including being able to write and decipher pure SQL. Significant experience with object-oriented programming techniques and best-practices. Significant experience with test-driven development (TDD) using Rspec. Experience writing your own JavaScript code (as well as working with JQuery), and the ability to roll your own HTML and CSS. Experience with modern source-control systems, specifically Git.

Topics that we'd expect you to be able to talk intelligently about include: testing and TDD, the strengths and weaknesses of ActiveRecord, the pros and cons of REST, why skinny controllers and skinny models are a Good Thing.

WHAT YOU'LL GET:
If you are the person we need - we know you are in high demand. We offer very competitive salaries, unlimited vacation time (seriously - we are only about results), free executive coaching and personal development, and equity. Our entire business is based on measuring and developing the behaviors that make people successful - so we REALLY value your design and personal development. Tell us how you want to grow, develop, influence and work. We always want to know. Our office is currently at Galvanize, but we'll be moving to a brand new space in RiNo early June in a complex that has LOTS of amenities: bike path, gym, steam room, heated outdoor pool, lots of community, restaurants, bike shop in building, beer, coffee and stuff to fuel your life and passions.

We want folks who enjoy working in a fast-paced, creative environment. You'll need to be able to demonstrate a capacity for learning new skills, a history of delivering work on time and on budget, and the ability to thrive under deadline pressure, which happens on occasion. Since we already have a product that you will be taking forward, you should also be comfortable diving into existing code and confidently deciding when and how to pragmatically refactor for optimal long-term value.

HOW TO APPLY:
Please write us a nice cover letter (yes, this is a test of your communication skills) and include other appropriate materials (e.g. code samples, GitHub repo, etc.) showcasing your experience, skills and passion.

Recruiters should not respond.
No contractors please, unless you are interested in a full-time, on-site position.
No phone calls, please.
