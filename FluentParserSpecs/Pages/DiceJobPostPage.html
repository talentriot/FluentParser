**6 MONTH CONTRACT TO HIRE**

Located in Glendale, CA, this global powerhouse hotel company has urgent growth plans within their mobile development team and is looking for an experienced iOS/iPhone developer! This is an opportunity for a Senior level developer to take the next step in their career! If you have experience with iOS Development since it has been out then please apply now!

What You Will Be Doing

Ideal candidate is:

Someone who has technically strong iOS expertise (with 2 to 3 years of iOS and more than 5 years of software engineering experience) and have completed several iPhone/iPad applications. He/she must have deep architectural and software design skills, well-versed in algorithms and data structures, and can write clean, maintainable code. He should also have experience leading and collaborating with offshore teams.

What You Need for this Position

- Programming experience in Objective-C for iOS for app development
- Strong understanding of interactive application development paradigms, memory management, file I/O, network & socket programming, concurrency and multi-threading
- Strong object-oriented design and development skills
- 2-4 years experience in the design and development (full lifecycle) of mobile applications (iOS)
- Ability to interface mobile applications with web applications
- Strong with Cocoa and Xcode (iOS) Environments
- Experience with Systems Architecture and Source Code
- Strong UI Design/Development
- Experience with Multi-threaded applications

Required:

- Bachelors degree in Computer Science or equivalent work experience
- Minimum 2-3 years hands-on experience in iOS

What's In It for You

-Highly Competitive Hourly rate
- Long term contract to potential FTE (salary)
-Work with some of the hottest technologyInterviews will be conducted this week and next week. Apply today!
